Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"
Innovation

A transformative era of digital, sustainable, and global innovation.

Sustainability

Advancing green tech, renewable energy, and eco-solutions.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure driving connectivity and growth

Empowerment

Empowering youth, women, and innovation for progress

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is a nation where poverty belongs to history, where every citizen thrives through innovation, entrepreneurship, and unity. It’s a society driven by the spirit of self-reliance, where technology and compassion coexist to create opportunities for all. Imagine a future where no one is left behind, where young minds redefine possibilities, and environmental sustainability is at the core of progress. Together, we can transform Bangladesh into a model for the world—a land of hope, dignity, and boundless potential."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
